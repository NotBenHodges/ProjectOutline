Ben and Daniel's CSS Project Design!

The goal of our project was to create a classical music
themed webpage that incorporates interactive piano keys.
To do this, we incorporated a hover effect on the piano keys
so that when the cursor moves over them, it appears that the
piano is being played. We also floated the grading and day
boxes so that the black keys would appear in between the white
ones, just like a real piano. We also included some colors that
we thought were appropriate for the theme, such as a dark brown
background and gray so that text is easier to read.
